About Us

This Project (FreshVision) is a collaborative effort by Students from SRM.
Our goal is to create an intuitive and efficient tool for detecting fruit freshness using advanced AI techniques.
We believe in the power of technology to enhance everyday experiences, and this project is a testament to that belief.

We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback from users to make this tool even better.
Thank you for using our Fruit Freshness Detector! We hope it serves you well in your fruit selection process.
